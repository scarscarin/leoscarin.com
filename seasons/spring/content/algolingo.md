# Algolingo
![image](../media/algolingo.png)

2023 


AlgoLingo is a game that, inspired by AlgoSpeak, forms a new language system. A language built from code words, with ever-changing secret rules, meme-related puns, and a lot of human creativity: something an algorithm could never understand!

The workshop AlgoLingo offers a new experience around language and freedom. Participants design their own coded language. This language may exist out of letters, colours and shapes. After designing and encoding messages, participant groups try to decipher the messages of others. Who is smart enough to decode the languages? And what are the implications of freedom of expression and privacy?
