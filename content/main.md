# Leo Scarin

![alt text](./media/leo.png)

**Leo Scarin** (he/him) is an artist and designer working with code. 

Engaged in interactive art as a means of commenting digital culture, Scarin's practice explores the social, political and increasingly ecological implications of technology. 

Based in [RGBdog studio](https://rgbdog.studio), at [KG95](https://kg95.nl), in The Hague, Scarin's practice spans between interactive media, moving image, and creative coding. 

Scarin is a lecturer in Interactive / Media / Design at KABK, in Moving Image at ArtEZ, and at the Interaction Station in WdKA.
