Leo Scarin

Leo Scarin is an artist and designer working with code. Based in RGBdog.studio, at KG95 in The Hague, Leo's practice stretches between interactive media, moving image, and creative coding -- adopting these disciplines to research and problematise technology's social, political, and ecological effects.
